# Expeditions And Adventures

## Definition

Expeditions are trips beyond the base region to find resources, POIs, bosses, biomes, or underground goals. Adventures are planned high-difficulty objectives with a specific target.

## Why It Matters

They are how research requirements become world movement, risk, logistics, combat, and discovery.

## Details

Expeditions:

- Search for new resources.
- Find points of interest.
- Find bosses.
- Explore dangerous biomes.
- Go underground.
- Support further research and crafting.

Adventures:

- Kill strong boss.
- Raid hunters.
- Clear dungeon.
- Retrieve rare resource from dangerous location.

Advanced research demands distant or specific resources, which creates:

- Outposts.
- Remote bases.
- Roads.
- Transport systems.
- Portals.
- Logistics between bases.
