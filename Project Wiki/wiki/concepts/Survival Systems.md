# Survival Systems

## Definition

Survival systems are early-game constraints around HP, stamina, hunger, thirst, and energy.

## Why It Matters

They create manual friction and resource pressure that later [[concepts/Magic System|magic]] can soften, automate, or bypass. This makes magical QoL feel earned.

## Details

Core stats:

- HP.
- Stamina.
- Later: mana.

Needs:

- Hunger: food restores it. Long neglect reduces max HP down to around 30%.
- Thirst: liquids restore it. Long neglect heavily slows stamina recovery.
- Energy: sleep restores it. Long neglect caps max stamina around 50%, later mana too.

Needs decay slowly, so player has a free-time buffer. [[concepts/Storage And Stockpiles|Stockpiles]] extend that buffer. [[concepts/Cooking|Cooking]] improves food value and can slow hunger decay.

Support systems:

- [[concepts/Gathering|Gathering]]
- [[concepts/Farming|Farming]]
- [[concepts/Hunting|Hunting]]
- [[concepts/Cooking|Cooking]]
- [[concepts/Storage And Stockpiles|Storage And Stockpiles]]

Magic can:

- Slow need decay.
- Restore needs.
- Overfill needs with special food/drinks.
- Maintain needs automatically.
- Fully bypass sleep, thirst, or hunger at high progression.
