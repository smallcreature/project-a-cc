# Bosses And Points Of Interest

## Definition

Bosses and points of interest are world challenges that test player progression and provide unique resources for further research.

## Why It Matters

They convert abstract power growth into concrete goals: defeat a boss, clear a dungeon, claim a rare material, unlock next research tier.

## Details

Source describes:

- Mandatory and optional bosses.
- Bosses tied to magic schools.
- Dungeons / points of interest with unique research resources.
- Early discovery of locations that are too hard until later progression.
- Planned adventures for high-value targets.
