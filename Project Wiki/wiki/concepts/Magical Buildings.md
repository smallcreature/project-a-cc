# Magical Buildings

## Definition

Magical buildings are functional structures created from block formations, often transformed into single special objects when their blueprint is matched.

## Why It Matters

They make construction part of magic instead of separating base-building from spell systems.

## Details

The game checks placed block formations against predefined building blueprints, including air blocks. If a formation fully matches, it can transform into a functional building.

Transformation types:

- Non-magical: happens immediately when formation matches.
- Magical: requires a special spell targeting the matching formation.

Examples:

- Ritual sites.
- Large resource processors: big furnace/smelter.
- Magical matrices for magical crafting.
