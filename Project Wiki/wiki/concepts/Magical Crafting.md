# Magical Crafting

## Definition

Magical crafting creates magical items using ingredients, [[concepts/Aura And Magical Energies|energies]], and [[concepts/Aspects|aspects]] on special workstations or structures.

## Why It Matters

It ties item power to infrastructure, room design, rituals, resource chains, and spell systems.

## Details

Crafting tier logic is capacity-based rather than artificial station locks:

- Primitive workstation: 2 ingredients, 1 aspect.
- Medium workstation: 4 ingredients, 2 aspects.
- Advanced structure: 9 ingredients, 4 aspects, ritual spell.

The strongest magical items require building special rooms/structures, placing recipe items, and performing ritual casting.

Potential outputs:

- Magical tools.
- Magical weapons.
- Magical equipment.
- Components for other magical recipes.
- Casting method items such as wands, staffs, grimoires, or orbs.
