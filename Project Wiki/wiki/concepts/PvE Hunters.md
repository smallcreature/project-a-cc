# PvE Hunters

## Definition

PvE hunters are adaptive NPC opponents that spawn near the player's camp after progression thresholds and operate by similar survival/resource rules.

## Why It Matters

They create external pressure, base defense needs, competition for resources, and raid/adventure targets.

## Details

Hunters:

- Spawn when player reaches certain progression/magic threshold.
- Compete for resources.
- Raid player's base.
- Can be raided by player.
- Provide unique resources.
- Make exploration harder because base can be attacked while player is away.
