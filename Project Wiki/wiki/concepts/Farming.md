# Farming

## Definition

Farming lets player grow food and alchemical ingredients using fertile soil, seeds, water, and a hoe.

## Why It Matters

It creates stable food supply, supports [[concepts/Survival Systems|survival]], feeds [[concepts/Cooking|cooking]], and gives optimization depth without blocking casual players.

## Details

Basic loop:

1. Place or find fertile soil.
2. Till with hoe.
3. Add fertilizer optional.
4. Plant seeds.
5. Water optional.
6. Wait.
7. Weed periodically.
8. Harvest.

Nutrients:

- Potassium: carrot, potato, flax.
- Phosphorus: pumpkin, onion, spelt.
- Nitrogen: cabbage, turnip, rye.

Crops consume nutrients. Repeated same-crop planting depletes one nutrient, slowing growth and yield. Nutrients restore over time or with fertilizer. Crop rotation lets depleted nutrients recover while other crop types grow.

Water speeds growth but is usually optional. Weeding is mandatory: weeds greatly slow growth.

Magic can:

- Speed growth, almost instantly at high power.
- Restore or overfill fertility.
- Automate watering.
- Automate weeding.
- Automate harvest.
- Automate reseeding later via golems.
