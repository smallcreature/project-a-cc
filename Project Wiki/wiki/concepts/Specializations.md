# Specializations

## Definition

Specializations are progression directions that let players develop distinct gameplay identities and divide labor in [[concepts/Deep Co-op|co-op]].

## Why It Matters

They support replayability, role identity, and group engagement. Different players can focus on different schools or infrastructure roles without all doing the same work.

## Details

Base set from source:

- 9 magic schools: pyromancy, hydromancy, geomancy, aeromancy, druidism, conjuration, chronotopy, aberrationism, arcana.
- Alchemy.
- 4 non-magic specializations: combat, farming, study, mining.

Each direction should have its own progression. In co-op, players can split schools or base roles: farming, research, mining, infrastructure, combat, exploration.
