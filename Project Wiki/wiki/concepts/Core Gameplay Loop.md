# Core Gameplay Loop

## Definition

Main loop: survive -> build -> progress -> research -> craft -> cast -> loot -> research.

## Why It Matters

Loop turns survival-craft activity into compounding discovery. Research creates reasons to explore, and exploration gives resources for more research.

## Details

The player:

1. Survives via [[concepts/Survival Systems|needs]], shelter, tools, and reserves.
2. Builds a base and infrastructure.
3. Gathers resources on surface, underground, and in other biomes.
4. Finds points of interest, bosses, dungeons, and dangerous biomes before being ready for them.
5. Hits recipe walls and needs [[concepts/Immersive Research|research]].
6. Uses research to unlock tools, workstations, spells, and magic.
7. Gains power and quality-of-life from [[concepts/Magic System|magic]].
8. Goes farther for rarer resources and planned [[concepts/Expeditions And Adventures|adventures]].

Progression naturally expands the map radius. Late research may require remote resources, which creates logistics pressure: outposts, roads, transport, portals, and multiple bases.
