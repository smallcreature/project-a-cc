# Aura And Magical Energies

## Definition

Aura is an invisible regional layer holding magical energies. Energies are world resources used by spells, magical crafting, and magical infrastructure.

## Why It Matters

Aura makes magic local and ecological. Spell use consumes and changes the environment instead of drawing only from player mana.

## Details

Regions contain different energy mixes based on biome:

- Forests: more nature.
- Water areas: more water.
- Other biomes likely emphasize corresponding energies.

Example energies:

- Fire.
- Water.
- Air.
- Earth.
- Nature.
- Arcane.
- Aberrational / corruption.

Energy states:

- Aura state: invisible numeric resource in region.
- Liquid: stored in liquid containers; usable in alchemy, crafting, recipes.
- Solid: crystals; portable crafting/spell resource.

Stored liquid/crystal energy is valuable because it holds one energy type and can be transported to regions where that energy is absent.

Energies recover over time. Buildings can accelerate recovery, saturate aura from stored energy, or later change the aura's composition.
