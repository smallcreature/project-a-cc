# Magic System

## Definition

Magic system is the central layer connecting spells, aura energies, aspects, mana, alchemy, magical crafting, magical buildings, automation, and corruption.

## Why It Matters

Magic is the main USP. It lets players simplify survival, increase power, automate routine work, specialize, and alter the world.

## Details

The player must manage:

- Personal mana.
- [[concepts/Aura And Magical Energies|Aura energies]] in region.
- Spell materials.
- [[concepts/Aspects|Aspects]] for alchemy and spell costs.
- [[concepts/Aberration And Corruption|Corruption]] from magical impact on environment.

Magic provides:

- Combat spells: projectiles, shields, weapons, status effects, buffs.
- Utility spells: plant growth, animal search, resource harvesting, terrain modification, instant cooking, time/weather control, movement.
- Magical tools/weapons: mining multiple blocks, weapon waves, special gathering rules.
- Magical equipment: double jump accessory, pickup ring, levitation boots, expanded backpack with built-in workstations.
- Magical workstations: fuel-less campfire, self-heating anvil, combined workstations.
- Magical buildings: rituals, aura cleaners, energy amplifiers, defensive pylons, levitating plates, processing and sorting buildings.
- Automation: resource processing, farming, resource generation, cooking, simple item-moving/crafting agents.
