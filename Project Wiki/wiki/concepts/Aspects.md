# Aspects

## Definition

Aspects are magical properties contained by items. They act like simplified magical chemistry and are used in alchemy, spell costs, and magical crafting.

## Why It Matters

Aspects connect mundane resources to magic. They let almost any item become meaningful in magical formulas.

## Details

Types:

- Primordial aspects: correspond to magical energies.
- Artificial aspects: represent complex concepts without matching aura energies.

Primordial aspects:

- Fire.
- Water.
- Earth.
- Air.
- Nature.
- Aberration.
- Arcana.

Artificial examples:

- Light.
- Dark.
- Life.
- Death.
- Time.
- Space.
- Mechanism.
- Metal.
- Tool.
- Destruction.

Example:

Spell "Rift" destroys terrain along a short line. It needs mana, earth aura energy, and destruction aspect. Since no destruction energy exists in aura, the spell consumes materials containing destruction aspect, such as cobblestones from mined rock.
