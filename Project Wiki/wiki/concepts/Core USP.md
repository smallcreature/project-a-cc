# Core USP

## Definition

Core USP of [[entities/Project Arcane|Project Arcane]] is a survival-craft game where magic is deep, systemic, consequential, and gradually lets the player automate or bypass survival-craft friction.

## Why It Matters

USP depends on the feeling that magic affects the whole game, not only combat. Player should feel like a researcher discovering rules, then exploiting them.

## Details

Key USP:

- Complex and honest [[concepts/Magic System|Magic System]] affecting survival, combat, infrastructure, and logistics.
- Many specializations: 9 magic schools, alchemy, combat, farming, study, mining.
- [[concepts/Immersive Research|Immersive Research]] with resource-property study and spell-formula discovery.
- Magical automation of survival, gathering, crafting, storage, transport, and production.
- [[concepts/Aberration And Corruption|Changing world]] caused by magic use.
- [[concepts/PvE Hunters|Adaptive PvE hunters]] that compete for resources and raid the player.
- Bosses and points of interest tied to progression.
- [[concepts/Deep Co-op|Deep co-op]] through role specialization.
