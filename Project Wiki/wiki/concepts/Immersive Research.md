# Immersive Research

## Definition

Immersive research is the process by which players study resources, infer properties, test hypotheses, and unlock recipes, tools, buildings, and spells through active systems rather than passive tech trees.

## Why It Matters

This supports the "magic scientist" fantasy. Player should discover rules step by step, not simply receive all mechanics as tutorialized facts.

## Details

Research appears in two broad forms:

- Mundane research: resource properties, survival recipes, tools, workstations, infrastructure.
- Magical research: spell formulas, aspects, aura energies, alchemy, magic crafting.

Research is intended to:

- Take time, resources, and player action.
- Send player on expeditions.
- Unlock new loops across farming, cooking, building, mining, hunting, storage, and magic.
- Reveal hidden rules such as [[concepts/Aura And Magical Energies|aura energies]], [[concepts/Aspects|aspects]], and [[concepts/Aberration And Corruption|corruption]].
