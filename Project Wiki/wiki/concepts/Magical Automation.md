# Magical Automation

## Definition

Magical automation is the use of spells, magical items, buildings, and agents to reduce or remove routine survival-craft labor.

## Why It Matters

It is the payoff for early friction. Player first feels manual work, then uses magic to simplify, bypass, or automate it.

## Details

Automation targets:

- Needs: slow, refill, overfill, or disable hunger/thirst/energy.
- Gathering: highlight resources, auto-pickup, filter pickups, gather radius.
- Farming: growth acceleration, fertility restoration, watering, weeding, harvesting, reseeding.
- Hunting: animal tracking, luring, population insight, animal creation.
- Cooking: portable cooking, simple/complex cooking automation, spoilage control.
- Storage: inventory unloading, sorting, capacity expansion, remote access.
- Resource processing: wood cutting, ore smelting, item movement, crafting triggers.

Possible late automation:

- Golems or primitive agents moving items and triggering craft.
- Magical sorting buildings.
- Multi-workstation magical processors.
