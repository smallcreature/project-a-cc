# Progression And Building

## Definition

Progression is the player's advancement across base scale, materials, resources, recipes, ores, tools, spells, and infrastructure. Building is the spatial foundation for workstations, storage, safety, and later magical structures.

## Why It Matters

The base is where survival-craft resources become durable capability. It hosts workstations, storage, research, crafting, rest, and magical infrastructure.

## Details

Progression markers:

- Scale/materials of buildings.
- Number of researched resources.
- Available recipes.
- Ore tiers.
- Tool quality.
- Learned spells.

Core mechanisms:

- Base expansion.
- Resource and recipe research.
- Spell research.
- Item crafting.

Workstation requirements:

- Minimum room volume.
- Enclosed room.
- Specific height/depth.
- Arcane energy in aura for magical workstations.

Optional bonuses:

- Specific room blocks/objects.
- Height/depth.
- Dominant aura energy.

Examples:

- Research workstation near library-like decorations improves favorable action chances.
- Food stored in a basement-like room gets preservation bonus.
