# Alchemy

## Definition

Alchemy transforms resources by dissolving their [[concepts/Aspects|aspects]] in a heated cauldron and consuming aspect combinations to produce new items.

## Why It Matters

Alchemy is a source of unique magical resources and a bridge between mundane items, aspects, magical crafting, and corruption.

## Details

Basic process:

1. Craft alchemical cauldron.
2. Add water.
3. Keep water heated with fuel.
4. Throw resource into cauldron.
5. Resource is destroyed and adds its aspects.
6. Throw target/catalyst item into cauldron.
7. Matching recipe consumes water and aspects.
8. Result item ejects near cauldron.

Aspects appear diegetically as icons levitating over cauldron when player approaches.

Unused aspects:

- Disappear when cauldron fully dries or is reset.
- Add aberrational energy to [[concepts/Aura And Magical Energies|aura]] based on quantity.

Example:

5 arcane aspects + 1 nature aspect + log -> arcane-infused wood. Unused earth aspect can become [[concepts/Aberration And Corruption|aberration]] if not spent.
