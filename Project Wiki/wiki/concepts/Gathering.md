# Gathering

## Definition

Gathering covers simple world resources collected by hand or tool without deeper mining/resource-node systems.

## Why It Matters

It supplies early survival, primitive tools, and a baseline ability to travel even when under-equipped.

## Details

Resource collection types:

- Dropped resources: pickups spawn after destroying resource object.
- Instant resources: item goes directly to inventory on interaction.

Tool differences:

- Gathering speed.
- Yield amount.
- Whether the resource can be gathered.

Early resources:

- Stones.
- Sticks.
- Plant fibers.
- Berries.
- Mushrooms.

Some resources have special spawn rules by biome or condition. Some have tool-specific behavior, such as plants regrowing when cut with a knife but disappearing if ripped by hand.

Magic can:

- Highlight needed resources.
- Auto-pickup items.
- Filter pickups.
- Gather resources in radius.
