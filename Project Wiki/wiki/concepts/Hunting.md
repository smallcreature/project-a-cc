# Hunting

## Definition

Hunting is animal-based food and material acquisition through killing and butchering wildlife.

## Why It Matters

It gives meat plus hides, fat, and bones for other crafting layers. It also introduces ecology through regional population limits.

## Details

Animal resources:

- Meat.
- Hides.
- Fat.
- Bones.

Tool examples:

- Skinning knife: more leather.
- Butcher cleaver: more meat and fat.

Population system:

- Each animal type has regional population limit.
- If current count is below limit and at least one male/female pair remains, animals reproduce.
- Offspring grow into adults over time.
- Players must avoid exterminating local populations.
- Animals can exceed limit by a tunable amount to make sustainable hunting easier.

Animal examples:

- Rabbits: tiny, easy, flee.
- Boars: medium, may counterattack.
- Deer: large, fast, best hunted with bow/slow effects.
- Wolves: dangerous, pack behavior, flee at low HP.
- Bears: solitary, durable, always counterattack.

Magic can:

- Track population.
- Find animals.
- Lure animals.
- Create animals.
